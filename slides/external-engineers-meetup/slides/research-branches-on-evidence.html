if data_anomaly   → inspect_warehouse_evidence()

You cannot enumerate every path through an unfamiliar codebase. Evidence at each step decides the next move. That is where agents become useful.

This bridges capability demos into the engine design: the agent needs to choose the next evidence path dynamically.
