Takeaway

Build the agent on top of the same playbooks humans already trust — then alignment becomes a measurable property, not a hope.

aim ≡ alignment
Closing thought: the long-term goal is an agent that investigates the way a human teammate would. The path there is not to invent a parallel agent-only playbook — it is to reuse the skills and context the teams already maintain manually for their own work. That gives us two things at once: the agent stays current as teams update their skills, and we get a measurable alignment test — does the agent's investigation land on the same evidence a human would?
