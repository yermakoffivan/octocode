Quality and measurements. Building an agentic flow isn't a one-time prompt — it requires constant evals and measurements. Six dials we watch: evals on every prompt and tool, measurements tracked per release, quality signals like citation rate and pivot rate, cost in tokens and tool calls, time end-to-end, and the KPIs that tie it all together. Without these, the system drifts silently.
