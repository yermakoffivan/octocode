Three intersecting axes of pressure — and you cannot ease one without aggravating another. Production surface: thousands of services and tens of thousands of repos, each with its own ownership, runtime, and observability. (This is the real enterprise gap: moving from workspace-only agents to org-wide, cross-repo indexing and permissions). Agentic velocity: thousands of engineers shipping in parallel, and now AI agents shipping alongside them — the target moves daily. Blast radius: millions of users — wrong answers ship to real people. The squeeze is the intersection: an answer that holds across all three. That is why we built an agentic research system.
